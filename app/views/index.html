Introduction to Develop Land and Property

I want to develop land and property covers the service area of building new homes.

The scope goes from land acquisition, through building and selling properties, to the disposal of the remaining parts of the land once all properties are sold.

Housing developers are not the only group of people and organisations involved in this area, with home buyers, their conveyancers, mortgage lenders, local authorities, Ordnance Survey and HM Land Registry playing important parts. These groups of users and organisations are really ecosystems of departments and further organisations, with other bodies like House Builders Federation also being involved.

The service area team has produced work on a variety of topics in this service area in the discovery, multiple alpha and beta phases.

Links to the production service and screenshots of each phase are available in the history below

  • Phase 4 Can we use CAD to create title plans Alpha - April to June 1019

    It was proven that title plans could be automated using digital plan drawings, in the current Mapping system

    75% of plots in the geo-located digital drawings, compared to extents drawn by caseworkers, were within the same tolerance we accept that caseworkers should draw to

    • Stages of drawing a title plan that could be automated
    • the range of CAD (digital drawings) received from developers
    • video demonstration of automating title plans from digital development plans

    Further significant technical work would be required to automate, at scale, title extents snapped to OS base maps, as caseworkers would do

    It was demonstrated that there is no technical, intellectual property, or other fundamental reason stopping developers sharing their digital plan drawings with HM Land Registry
